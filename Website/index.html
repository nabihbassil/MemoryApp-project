Overview

About

You are gathering many souvenirs from past trips but over time you cannot find them anymore or they get simply lost? Also you would like to get reminded now and then by a certain souvenir? Then the memory map is your best choice. After inserting on this web application where and when you are going on vacation the corresponding country-box in your memory map will automatically open up.

Having inserted your souvenir and pushed in the box, a boarder light will light up and indicate your visit. After a predefined time the box will light up from behind, your souvenir is projected at the transparent front and the box will open up. Have a look, soak up some happy memories and close the box again.
